Our Privacy Policy

This privacy policy pertains to the handling of information, data, and any assets collected by Sapidex or provided to Sapidex at the sole discretion of its clients.

This privacy policy does not encompass the practices of companies and third parties that Sapidex does not own or control, or individuals whom Sapidex does not employ or manage, including any third-party content contributors under contract.

The term “Information,” as used herein, denotes any information voluntarily provided by our clients through any means for the execution of work as per the business contract. Sapidex reserves the right to amend this policy at its sole discretion, without prior notice. Please maintain the latest version of this policy for reference.

This privacy policy aims to comply with privacy laws applicable to the personal data collected and used during engagements with our customers and clients.

Information Collection and Use

Sapidex values the privacy of its customers and clients. This policy, on behalf of Sapidex and its global offices, hereinafter referred to as “Sapidex,” outlines the collection, storage, and processing of information. Sapidex retains the right to occasionally modify this policy at its discretion.

Information Collection

All customer/client information collected will be used for the purpose intended as per the agreement. Customer information, including but not limited to Non-Public Information (NPI) and Personally Identifiable Information (PII), will be protected using appropriate controls and security measures (such as usernames and passwords) and shared between Sapidex and its clients.

Sapidex collects information such as names, emails, shipping addresses, and financial information like credit card numbers and expiration dates via web-based order forms from clients/customers ordering our products. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill customer orders. If there are issues with order processing, the information is used to contact clients/customers.

Information Use

Sapidex utilizes collected information as part of engagements to:

  • Provide clients/customers with information regarding product upgrades, new products, services, newsletters, informative emails, and research on future product ideas or enhancements.
  • Assist Sapidex in creating relevant content for clients/customers.
  • Aid Sapidex in developing better products and services to meet clients/customers’ needs.
  • Enable clients/customers to purchase, download, and manage product licenses, gain access to services, or engage in selected activities.
  • Help Sapidex share software, services, or product information important to its clients/customers.

Cookies

Sapidex uses cookies, which are pieces of data stored on users’ computers linked to user information. These cookies, specifically session ID cookies, are not connected to personally identifiable information while on our site. Session ID cookies terminate once users close their browsers. Cookies are employed to maintain user identity after login, allowing users to log in once per web session. You can choose to disable cookies following your browser’s instructions. However, this may impact site accessibility based on your location within our site.

Disclosure of Information

Except as described in this privacy policy, we will not, without clients/customers’ prior consent, sell information, such as Non-Public Information (NPI) and Personally Identifiable Information (PII), to third parties other than authorized third parties and others involved in the e-commerce distribution chain (e.g., vendors, suppliers) who may require access to some personal information for order fulfillment.

Communication of Information

Sapidex is committed to preserving user privacy. However, under certain circumstances, Sapidex may need to disclose personal information when required by law or when there is a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our products, services, or website.

Links to External Sites

Sapidex provides links to other sites on its websites as part of services to different clients. Sapidex is not responsible for the privacy practices of such external sites. We encourage clients/customers to review the privacy statements of each website that collects personally identifiable information, whether voluntarily or involuntarily redirected to external sites. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by Sapidex during its engagements with customers and clients.

Security Measures

Whenever Sapidex collects sensitive information like credit card numbers and billing details, it is encrypted and protected with industry-standard encryption technology. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure proper use of information, Sapidex has implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard the information collected.

Minors/Children's Privacy

Sapidex, in consideration of the privacy of personal information received from minors/children (individuals under the age of 18), will not knowingly receive/collect personal information through company portals and websites. If Sapidex becomes aware of any personal information received from a minor available on company portals and websites, it will be promptly deleted.

Updating Personal Information

If a customer’s or client’s personally identifiable information changes (e.g., zip code, phone number, email, or postal address), or if a client discontinues using our services, Sapidex will correct, update, or delete the user’s personally identifiable information.

Sapidex will notify users of any changes to its privacy policy. These changes will be posted in this privacy statement, on our website, and other appropriate locations. This ensures our users, customers, and clients are informed about the information we collect, how it is used, and any disclosures made under specific circumstances. In the event that any provisions of this privacy policy are deemed unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain unaffected, and the unenforceable provision(s) will be replaced with enforceable provision(s) that align with the original intention.
